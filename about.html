Our Story

From Guwahati to the world - Premium clothing for the modern man

Born in Guwahati

FitPickd was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create premium clothing that fits perfectly and feels exceptional. Our journey began in the vibrant city of Guwahati, where tradition meets modernity, and craftsmanship is celebrated.

We believe that great style starts with the perfect fit. That's why every piece in our collection is designed with attention to detail, ensuring that you not only look good but feel amazing in what you wear.
